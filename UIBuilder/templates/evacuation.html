Quick checklist

General habits when a storm threatens — follow official orders and closures.

  2. Match the zone map below to your county’s published evacuation map.
  3. Pack go-bags, meds, documents, and pet supplies early.
  4. Review nearby traffic and FL511 before you choose a route.
